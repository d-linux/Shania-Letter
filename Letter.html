A journey through my heart and soul.

Unfold My Love

Dear Shania,

From the very first moment I met you,
I felt something I couldn't explain
a warmth, a pull, a quiet spark
that felt both comforting and terrifying.
There was a connection between us
that shook the walls I'd built around my heart.
I tried to protect myself,
but the truth is, the more I tried to resist,
the deeper I fell in love with you.
And by the time I realised it,
it was already too late
my heart was completely yours.

I can't recall a single day
when you haven't made me feel loved.
Even in our storms
in the disagreements, the silence, the tears
you've always found a way
to remind me of your love.
You've loved me with a patience,
a tenderness,
that I never thought I deserved.

God truly blessed me with you.
Your presence in my life
is proof that He listens,
that He knows exactly what my soul needs.
You've shown me that life is still beautiful,
that love can be gentle and strong
all at once.

When I think of you,
I'm filled with a joy so pure
it feels like sunlight in my chest.
Sometimes I wonder how someone like you
could ever love someone like me
but then I see how our hearts fit together,
how our differences make us stronger,
and I know we were meant to be.

You are my answered prayer,
my peace, my home.
Without you, life would be a restless dream,
but with you,
I'm living the kind of dream
I never want to wake from.

Made with all my love, Denis
